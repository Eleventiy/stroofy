How our bookkeeping service works

We get you set up

First, we’ll introduce you to a dedicated bookkeeper. They’ll get to know your business, show you how Bench works, and gather everything they need from you.

We do your bookkeeping

Each month, your bookkeeper imports your business transactions and gets to work on your books. If they need anything from you, they’ll get in touch.

We deliver intuitive financial reports

Real-time and monthly financial reporting let you track your business health and performance. At year end, we send a tidy financial package for tax time.
